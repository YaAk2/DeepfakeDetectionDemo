Welcome to the DFirt page!
Here you can upload images, videos, paste URLs to images or videos and classify if it's a deepfake!

How it works ...

Firstly, a face is detected on your sample using a face detector.
Secondly, the detected face is cropped, resized and fed through the deepfake classifier.

Dataset

The training set consisted of FaceForensics++ dataset, DFFD dataset, DFDC dataset, google's deepfake dataset.
These datasets were combined and augmented further. As preprocessing step only cropping and resizing of the samples were used.

Deepfake Classifier

The model to detect faces is a pretrained MTCNN. To classify between deepfakes and real an EfficientNet-B0 is deployed.
